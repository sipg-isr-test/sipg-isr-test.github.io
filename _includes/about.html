About

Our work spans both theoretical and applied domains, with applications in areas such as:

Wireless communications

Blind equalization, source separation, and signal processing for modern wireless systems.

Underwater systems

Research on underwater acoustic communications, video compression, and data analysis in marine environments.

Time-frequency analysis

Non-stationary signal modeling and analysis using advanced time-frequency methods.

Image processing

Statistical modeling, denoising, segmentation, and feature extraction for visual data.

Video analysis

Motion estimation, deformable model tracking, and inference of 3D structure from video.
